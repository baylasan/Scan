WHY SCANY ?

Welcome to SCANY, your all-in-one QR code solution. Whether you’re a business owner, marketer, or an individual looking to streamline your digital interactions, SCANY is here to help. With our intuitive QR code generator, you can effortlessly create custom codes for a wide range of applications.
Say goodbye to traditional methods and unlock a world of possibilities with SCANY.

Your custom QR code

SCANY QR codes can stores a multiple data options such as your contact details, menu, images and video, URL, and much more.

Sharing has never been easier

Anyone with a smartphone can scan your SCANY QR code to keep in touch!

Add it to your lock-screen

Create a lock-screen background image with your personal QR Code for even faster access.

Your data is secure

All data is encrypted and stored in secure and certified hosting providers. You can also choose the enterprise license and store your data on your premises!

HOW IT WORKS?

1. Choose the QR Type

Select the type QR code that you would like to create and share, Like a digital business card, menu, web site link, file, etc..

2. Add Your Content

Add your custom content that you would like to share, Noting that you can update these content any time easily!

3. Share Your QR code

Get your QR code in any format, Print it or share it digitally. It’s as easy as that!
